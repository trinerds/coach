This document tracks support tickets that are valid product ideas but should not stay in the engineering bug backlog.

It exists so we can:

- keep bug triage focused on defects and regressions
- preserve user-requested product ideas instead of losing them in closed support tickets
- give support and product a lightweight place to review demand clusters later
